INTERNSHIP OBJECTIVES

Doing an internship

During the second year of your DUT you’ll have to do an internship in a company during your final semester, some people may wonder why they have to do this so let me show you some reasons of our doing.

  • An internship is the occasion to observe profesionnals in working situation and thanks to that, discover new aspects that you didn’t know about this profession and to check if you really are made for this job.

  • In case you know what profession you want to excerce but you don’t know where to practice it ; the internship is a good way to discover it and helping you settle in a work place that you ‘ll appreciate.

  • Doing multiple internships give you the advantage to know a lot about work so you get a lot of experience which is very appreciated by the recruiters nowadays so it’ll help you get a job.

  • Finally, depending on the type of internship you’re doing you can also be remunerated so it can help you getting some revenue while you work for a future job.

Going abroad

The university allows you to go abroad and do an internship (or study at a foreign university) in a country of your choice, among the available ones. This gives out many possibilities:

  • For the first time of your life, you can experience being independant and having to do everything by yourself. That is a great way to become more autonomous.

  • You will also discover a new way to live, a new culture by living for a few months there. You can choose between many countries around the world: Japan, Canada, Vietnam, Poland... every country has a different aspect, so choose you know you prefer!

  • And of course, you still get a good taste of what working as an adult is like.
